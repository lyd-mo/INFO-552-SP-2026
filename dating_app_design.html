"What Are We? Dating App Design, Communication, and the Search for Meaningful Connections"

We've all heard it: "the apps" are ruining romance. But is that actually true ? And if so, whose fault is it?

I'd argue the design is doing a lot of damage. For this research project, I explored whether dating platforms could be better built to help users actually communicate. Specifically: can good design help people move past surface-level small talk and signal romantic intent more clearly?

To understand this better, I examined at how relationship development theories like the Social Penetration Theory and Uncertainty Reduction Theory might offer a blueprint for better dating app design.
